Our wood chips are small to medium-sized fragments derived from natural wood sources. We specialize in exporting top-grade wood chips processed from sustainably harvested timber and sawmill byproducts. Designed to meet global standards, our wood chips are ideal for use in biomass energy, paper production, and landscaping.

With a strong commitment to consistency and quality, we ensure each shipment meets exact specifications — low moisture, uniform size, and export-ready. We offer flexible bulk solutions tailored to your specific industry needs, whether for industrial fuel, pulp processing, or agricultural applications.

Our streamlined logistics — from on-site processing to port containerization — guarantees on-time global delivery. Backed by robust supply chain networks and professional export management, we’re your trusted partner for sustainable and scalable wood chip supply.
